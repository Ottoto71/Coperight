Hope dies last

"Hope dies last" is a powerful and evocative phrase, often used to convey the idea that no matter how dire or difficult the situation may seem, hope clings to life longer than anything else. It suggests that even in the face of overwhelming adversity, there is a part of us that refuses to give up, that holds on to the belief that things can get better, or that a breakthrough is just around the corner.
